About

I am a Biomedical Engineering and Computer Science Junior at Duke University who enjoys building and tinkering with the hardware and software of medical devices. I have obtained most of my hardware and software knowledge from my past projects including a wearable breathalyzer, an AI squat assist device, and a smart water coaster. In the future, I am interested in exploring innovative AI and engineering techniques to solve medical problems in the medical device industry and promoting accessibility to healthcare.

  • Technical Skills: Microcontrollers, Circuit Design (KiCad), PCB Layout, Soldering, OnShape, Fusion 360
  • Languages: Python, C, Java, MATLAB, JavaScript, SQL, C#/.NET
  • Frameworks & Tools: Git, TensorFlow, PyTorch, OpenCV
